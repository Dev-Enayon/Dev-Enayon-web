About Me

My journey as a web developer began when I built my first website using HTML and CSS. I was just exploring out of curiosity, but I quickly realized how much I enjoyed bringing ideas to life on the web. That small project sparked a deep interest in coding and design, and since then, I’ve been passionate about building websites and web applications that are both useful and enjoyable to use. Over the years, I’ve worked hard to learn and grow my skills. I’ve become confident working with both frontend and backend technologies, including React, Next.js, Node.js, and MongoDB. These tools help me build modern, responsive websites that work well on any screen size and are easy for users to navigate. I also pay close attention to performance and accessibility, making sure that everyone can use the websites I build, no matter their device or ability. One of the things I love most about web development is the mix of creativity and problem-solving. I enjoy building clean and simple user interfaces, developing APIs that connect different parts of an app, and finding better ways to improve user experience. I'm especially interested in modern JavaScript frameworks and enjoy experimenting with new tools and libraries to stay up to date. I believe learning never stops in tech, so I’m always looking for opportunities to grow—whether it’s through online courses, reading articles, or building side projects. I’m excited to continue my journey as a web developer, take on new challenges, and create solutions that make a positive impact.
